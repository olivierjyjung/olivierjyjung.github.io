About

A portrait of the researcher

O. J. Jung, 2026 Seoul, KR

I’m a master’s student at Ewha Womans University studying how AI is changing the way people communicate and interact in public spaces. My work sits at the intersection of Human–AI Interaction and Computational Social Science, where I use tools like speech modeling and natural language processing to explore how AI shapes trust, perception, and conversation.

I’m especially interested in building AI systems that help people communicate in more constructive and inclusive ways.
