I examine how AI systems reshape human communication, media trust, and public discourse.

Olivier Jiyoun Jung is a graduate researcher in Communication & Media at Ewha Womans University. Bridging computational social science and multimodal deep learning, her research examines how AI systems reshape human communication, media trust, and public discourse.

She combines methods across speech, vision, and text to investigate authenticity perception, AI-mediated dialogue, and the societal impact of synthetic media.
